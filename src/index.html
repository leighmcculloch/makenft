An NFT will be created on the Stellar network by publishing the information entered here to the public Stellar network. If a file is uploaded to Stellar it will be permanently stored on the public Stellar network. If a file is uploaded to IPFS is may be ephemeral and disappear at anytime. In both cases it will not be possible to delete the file.

Your account will pay the issuing account reserve and all transactions fees. The reserve will not be reclaimable.

Once the NFT is created you can send or sell your NFT using your wallet, or DEX app.
